Pork Caldereta

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork shoulder or pork belly, cubed
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water or beef broth
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 carrot, peeled and cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 1 cup liver spread
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Sauté minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant.
  2. Add cubed pork shoulder or pork belly, and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce and water or beef broth. Add dried bay leaves and paprika. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 30 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  5. Add cubed potatoes, carrots, sliced red and green bell peppers, and green peas. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Once the vegetables are cooked, add liver spread and stir until well incorporated.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  9. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
