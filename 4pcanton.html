Pancit Canton

Ingredients:

  • 250g pancit canton noodles
  • 200g pork, sliced into strips
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion.
  2. Add pork and shrimp. Cook until slightly browned.
  3. Stir in carrots and bell peppers.
  4. Add chicken broth, soy sauce, and oyster sauce.
  5. Bring to a boil then add pancit canton noodles. Cook until noodles are soft.
  6. Serve hot.
