Beef Bulalo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef shank with bone marrow (bulalo cut)
  • 4 pieces corn on the cob, cut into thirds
  • 4 pieces pechay (bok choy)
  • 2 pieces medium-sized potatoes, quartered
  • 1 piece onion, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 tablespoon whole peppercorns
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges

Steps:

  1. In a large pot, add enough water to cover the beef shank. Bring to a boil and skim off any scum that rises to the surface.
  2. Add the quartered onion, crushed garlic, and whole peppercorns. Simmer for 2-3 hours or until the beef is tender.
  3. Once the beef is tender, add the quartered potatoes and cook for another 10-15 minutes or until the potatoes are soft.
  4. Season with fish sauce and salt to taste.
  5. Add the corn on the cob and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Add the pechay (bok choy) and cook for another 2 minutes or until the vegetables are tender yet still crisp.
  7. Transfer the beef bulalo into serving bowls. Garnish with chopped spring onions.
  8. Serve hot with calamansi or lemon wedges on the side.
