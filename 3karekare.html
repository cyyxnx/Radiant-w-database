Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 eggplants, sliced
  • 1 bundle string beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 banana blossom (sliced and boiled)
  • 2 tablespoons annatto seeds (achuete)
  • 4 cups water
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste

Steps:

  1. Boil oxtail in water until tender. Set aside.
  2. In a separate pan, sauté garlic and onion.
  3. Add boiled oxtail, peanut butter, and annatto seeds diluted in water.
  4. Simmer until sauce thickens.
  5. Add eggplants, string beans, and banana blossom. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with fish sauce.
  7. Serve hot with bagoong (shrimp paste) on the side.
