Pork Sisig

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, boiled and chopped
  • 1 large onion, minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3-4 pieces green chili peppers (siling haba), chopped
  • 2 pieces red chili peppers (siling labuyo), chopped (optional)
  • 1 knob ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup calamansi juice
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped spring onions for garnish
  • Fried garlic bits for garnish
  • Egg (optional), for topping

Steps:

  1. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add chopped pork belly and cook until slightly crispy.
  4. Add green chili peppers and red chili peppers (if using), and continue to cook for a few minutes.
  5. Season with soy sauce, calamansi juice, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  6. Add mayonnaise and mix until well combined.
  7. Transfer the sisig to a sizzling plate, if available, and top with chopped spring onions and fried garlic bits.
  8. If desired, crack an egg on top of the sisig and let it cook from the residual heat.
  9. Serve hot as an appetizer or main dish.
