Pakbet

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups squash, sliced
  • 2 cups eggplant, sliced
  • 1 cup okra
  • 1 cup string beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. In a pan, sauté garlic, onion, and tomato.
  2. Add squash and cook until slightly tender.
  3. Add eggplant, okra, and string beans.
  4. Stir in shrimp paste and cook until vegetables are cooked.
  5. Serve hot.
