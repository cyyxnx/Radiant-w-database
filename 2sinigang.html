Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork ribs or shrimp
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish, sliced
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 10 pieces string beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 packet sinigang mix (tamarind soup base)

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a pot and add pork ribs or shrimp, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Simmer until meat is tender.
  3. Add radish, eggplant, and string beans. Simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  4. Stir in sinigang mix and adjust seasoning if needed.
  5. Serve hot.
