Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup green onions, chopped
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Spring roll wrappers
  • Oil for frying
  • Sweet and sour sauce or banana ketchup for dipping

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground pork, chopped carrot, chopped onion, minced garlic, chopped green onions, beaten egg, soy sauce, salt, and ground black pepper. Mix until well combined.
  2. Place a spoonful of the pork mixture onto a spring roll wrapper and spread it out horizontally. Roll the wrapper tightly, folding in the sides halfway through. Seal the edge with a bit of water.
  3. Repeat the process until all the pork mixture is used up.
  4. Heat oil in a deep pan or fryer over medium heat.
  5. Fry the lumpiang Shanghai in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 5-7 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove from the oil and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce or banana ketchup for dipping.
