Bicol Express

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly, sliced
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5 pieces red chili peppers (siling labuyo), chopped
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • Cooking oil

Steps:

  1. In a pan, heat cooking oil and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add pork belly slices and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in shrimp paste and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in coconut milk and add chopped chili peppers.
  5. Simmer until pork is tender and the sauce thickens.
  6. Adjust seasoning according to taste.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
