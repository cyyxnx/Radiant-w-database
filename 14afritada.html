Chicken Afritada

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken pieces (legs, thighs, or breast), cut into serving pieces
  • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 2 potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 carrot, peeled and sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 3 pieces dried bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large pot or pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Sauté minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce and chicken broth. Add dried bay leaves and paprika. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add quartered potatoes, sliced carrots, sliced red and green bell peppers, and green peas. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Once the vegetables are cooked and the sauce has thickened, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
