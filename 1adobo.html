Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken or pork (or combination of both), cut into serving pieces
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil

Steps:

  1. In a pot, combine chicken or pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  2. Bring to a boil then simmer until meat is tender.
  3. Heat oil in a separate pan and fry the meat until browned.
  4. Serve hot with rice.
