Pork Menudo

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork shoulder or pork belly, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup pork broth
  • 1 potato, peeled and cubed
  • 1 carrot, peeled and cubed
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1/2 cup raisins
  • 3 pieces hotdogs, sliced diagonally
  • 2 pieces dried bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Steps:

  1. In a large pot or pan, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Sauté minced garlic and chopped onion until fragrant.
  2. Add cubed pork shoulder or pork belly, and cook until lightly browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce and pork broth. Add dried bay leaves. Stir to combine.
  4. Bring to a simmer and let it cook for about 30 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  5. Add cubed potatoes, cubed carrots, diced bell pepper, green peas, raisins, and sliced hotdogs. Simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Once the vegetables are cooked and the sauce has thickened, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
