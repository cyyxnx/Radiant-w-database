Pancit Palabok

Ingredients:

  • 250g rice noodles (bihon)
  • 200g shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 200g ground pork
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cups shrimp stock (or water)
  • 2 tablespoons annatto powder
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce (patis)
  • 1 cup pork cracklings (chicharon)
  • Boiled eggs, sliced
  • Chopped green onions
  • Fried garlic
  • Calamansi or lemon wedges
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cook rice noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add ground pork and cook until browned. Add shrimp and cook until pink.
  4. Pour in shrimp stock and bring to a simmer. Add annatto powder for color.
  5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper.
  6. Arrange cooked noodles on a serving plate. Pour the shrimp and pork mixture on top.
  7. Garnish with pork cracklings, boiled eggs, chopped green onions, and fried garlic.
  8. Serve hot with calamansi or lemon wedges on the side.
