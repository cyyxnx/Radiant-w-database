Tinola

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green papaya, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Chili leaves or spinach

Steps:

  1. In a pot, sauté ginger, garlic, and onion.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until slightly browned.
  3. Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add green papaya and simmer until tender.
  5. Season with fish sauce.
  6. Add chili leaves or spinach before serving.
  7. Serve hot.
